Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What makes Neczux different from other service providers?

We're not a fragmented service provider — we're an integrated home provider. All our services work as one system, eliminating the need to manage multiple vendors and ensuring seamless integration across your business operations. Strategy, technology, design, and training all speak the same language here.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. Our integrated approach and proven frameworks typically deliver results 2× faster than traditional methods. A standard website project takes 2–4 weeks, while comprehensive business solutions may take 4–8 weeks. We'll give you a precise timeline during consultation.

Can I start with one service and add more later?

Absolutely. Many clients begin with one pillar — such as a website or brand identity — and gradually integrate other services as they grow. Our architecture is designed to be modular yet interconnected, so every addition builds on what's already in place.

Do you work with early-stage startups or only established businesses?

We work with both. Our services are designed to support businesses at any stage — from founders with an idea to operational companies looking to scale. The right entry point depends on where you are, and we'll help you figure that out in our first conversation.

How does billing and payment work?

Every engagement is tailored to the scope of work. We provide a clear quote before any work begins, and payment structures are discussed and agreed upon upfront. We accept bank transfers, mobile money, and selected online payment methods.

What is your response time for inquiries?

We respond to all inquiries within 24 hours (usually much sooner during business hours). For urgent matters, WhatsApp or phone calls are the fastest way to reach us.
