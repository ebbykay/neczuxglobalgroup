Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly — your name, email address, phone number, company name, and any message content submitted through our contact form or newsletter signup.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to respond to your enquiries, provide services you have requested, send business updates you have opted into, and improve our services. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience. You can accept or decline non-essential cookies via the cookie banner when you first visit. Essential cookies required for site function are always active.

4. Data Storage

Form submissions are processed by Formspree and stored in our Airtable CRM. Both services are GDPR-compliant. Data is retained for no longer than 3 years unless an active client relationship exists.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data at any time. Contact us at info@neczuxglobal.com to exercise these rights.
